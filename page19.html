Quick naps

Because a quick nap is good for your health.

Quick naps can be set in one click! A quick nap is an alarm that is not set to ring at a specific time, but after a specific duration.

When you set a Quick Nap, you select a duration, and your alarm will go off to wake you up on time!

  • CUSTOMIZABLE DURATIONS - By going into the Quick nap section, in the Preferences (Preferences than Alarm section), you can customize up to 5 different durations that will be available to set your quick naps.Try it now!
  • QUICK NAP SETTINGS ARE DEFAULTS - A quick nap is automatically created using default settings. But, don't worry! Quick naps have default settings different from standard alarms, so you can easily customize those!Try it now

Create Quick Naps from nearly everywhere

Multiple ways are at your disposal to easily create Quick Naps.

  1. FROM THE APPLICATION - When you click on the button used to create a new alarm, you can choose instead to create a quick nap.Try it now!
  2. WIDGETS - Widgets can be added to your homescreen to create Quick Naps. Custom durations are not available for the widget.ry it now!
  3. APPLICATION SHORTCUTS - A Quick Nap option is available with the Application Shortcuts (or Launcher Shortcuts) that permits to create a Quick Nap.Try it now!
