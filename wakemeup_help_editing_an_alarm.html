Wake me up! Smart Alarm Clock is an Android application that can be used as:

  • An alarm clock
  • A stopwatch
  • A timer
  • A nightstand clock
It can be integrated with Tasker, works with Wear OS and completely supports standard battery optimizations for Android 5 and higher.
