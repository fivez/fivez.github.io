Sound customization

Like for everything else within Wake me up!, customize what you will hear when waking up!

Ringtone, text-to-speech and music files

Multiple options are there to help you wake up in a better mood!

Ringtones

Obviously, all your ringtones can be used with Wake me up!

They will ring endlessly, until your alarm is either snoozed, dismissed or missed.
Just select the "Silent" ringtone if you wish for your alarm to remain silent.

Text-to-speech

Create your own message that will be said by your alarm, instead of a ringtone!

Your message does not need to be static!
Use variables to customize it, so that you hear what time is it, the date or even the events of the day on your calendar!

Text-to-speech then ringtone

Create a unique message that will be said once, and then hear your ringtone that will repeat until the alarm is either snoozed, missed or dismissed.

Music file

Use your own music files as ringtone!

The music file needs to be stored locally on the device.

Help your better half sleep longer

Wake me up! recognizes when you take your phone and can mute the ringtone or decrease the ringtone volume if you wish.
