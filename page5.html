Snooze options

Because sometimes you need a little more time, you can snooze your alarm so that it rings again later.

With Wake me up!, snooze your alarm the way you want.

Change the delay after which your alarm will ring again per alarm! Because your alarm are set for different needs, you may need one alarm to be snoozed for 1 minute and another one for 10 minutes.
With Wake me up!, you can just do it.

Decreasing snooze length

Change the snooze length depending on the number of time your alarm has been snoozed!
You've snoozed your alarm for 10 minutes because you need more time.
If you've set the decreasing snooze length to 2 minutes, the next time your alarm will ring will me in 8 minutes, then 6 minutes, 4 minutes, 2 minutes, and then every minute.

Make sure you won't snooze too often

Limit the number of time you can snooze your alarm! Because, you cannot postpone indefinitely, make sure that you won't be able to snooze your alarm after snoozing it already a number of time you define.

Or even forbid snoozing! If you think your alarm is that important, just forbid it to be snoozed. You won't have another choice than to dismiss it.

Make sure you don't miss your alarm

Some alarms are more important than others.

You can define the time after which an alarm is considered missed. With Wake me up!, this time can be set on a per alarm basis.

And if your alarm is really important, you can even set it to never be missed!

Snooze automatically an alarm that should have been missed!

Doing so, you'll make sure that you will have another chance to hear it!
Of course, you can set a maximum number of time it will be automatically snoozed, after which it will considered missed.
And, because Wake me up! is all about flexibility, why not making your alarm ring longer on its last repeat?

Movements

Multiple movements can be used to snooze your alarm:
- By flipping your phone
- By waving the hand three times over your phone.
- By shaking your phone
