Text-to-speech

Your alarm can tell you all you need after you dismiss it.

Multiple options have been made available for you so that you can hear the information that matter to you, when you dismiss your alarm.
  1. GREETINGS - There's no reason for your alarm to be impolite. So, if you'd like, your alarm can greet you.Try it now!
  2. DATE - Your alarm can also tell you the date of the day, including, if you'd like, the name of the day. Or, it can tell you only the name of the day. The choice is yours.
  3. TIME - Your alarm can tell you the time, in case you're used to snooze your alarm and you need a reminder of how late you are.
  4. WEATHER FORECAST - Your alarm can tell you the weather forecastifor the day, as a brief summary.
  5. TEMPERATURE - Your alarm can tell you the minimum and maximum forecasted temperature for the day.
  6. CALENDAR EVENTS - Your alarm can read you the time and label for each event you have on the day, for all calendars you've set the application to synchronize with.
